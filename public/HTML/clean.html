Step 1: Remove device lid and remove lid of desired container.
Step 2: Remove desired container and clean with soap and water.
Step 3: Add water to container and place back in device with lid. The water will be used to flush the line.
Step 4: Use buttons to flush line for desired tank. Hold the button until the liquid coming out of the nozzle is clean
Step 5: Now remove the water from the container and add you beverage and place the beverage in the device.
Step 6: Hold the button for the tank with the new fluid until the water is no longer in the line
